Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

For Prep
  • 1lb smoked gouda
  • 1/2lb sharp cheddar
  • 4 slices sourdough
  • 1 pat butter
For Serving
  • Fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper
  • Panera Tomato Basil soup

Steps

  1. Heat pan
  2. Add butter
  3. Add 2 slices of bread
  4. Grate and insert cheese
  5. Add other slices on top
  6. Flip sandwich when bottom is golden brown
  7. Add lid
  8. Remove sandwiches and eat
